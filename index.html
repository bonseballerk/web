The capital is Paris.

The capital of my stydy abroad destination Caen.

France is a historic country located in Western Europe. It takes about 12 hours and 45 minutes from Japan. The population is about 67 million and the area is 5511,000㎢. The French mainland is also called "hexagon" because it has a hexagonal shape. As the capital, Paris, is known as the most touristy city in the world, France has many historical buildings and many tourist attractions.

My recommended places

Here, I’d like to introduce carefully selected tourist attractions to go to when you go to France.
If you are going to France, please go there.

La tour Eiffel

The Eiffel Tower

It was built for the Paris International Exposition in 1889. However, at that time, the citizens of Paris were fiercely opposed because of eccentricity. Now, 7 million people visit a year, making it the largest symbol of Paris.

Musée du Louvre

Louvre Museum

It is a must-visit attraction when visiting Paris. There are many famous paintings and sculptures on display, including Milovinas, Nike and the Mona Lisa written by Da Vinci.

Opéra

Opera

It was built in 1875 by the French architect Charles Garnier. It is a baroque building, one of the most beautiful and luxurious. Operas and ballet performances are common here. You can enter just by visiting the museum.

University of Caen Normandy

Caen University is a historic university founded by Henry VI in 1432. Located in northern France, facing England across the sea, Caen, one of France's leading castle towns, is lined with historic buildings such as Caen Castle, known as one of the largest medieval citadels in Western Europe. It takes about 2 hours by express train from Paris Saint-Lazare station.
